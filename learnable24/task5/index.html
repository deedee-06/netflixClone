Prairie Dog

13

* Prairie dogs are barrowing rodents living in the grassland of western and central North America.

*Their underground colonies provide habitats for nearly 150 other species.

*Prairie dogs have a collection of high-pitched barks that signal the presence of specific enemies.

Mockingbird

9

* Mockingbirds get their name from their ability to mimic the sounds of animals and other noises.

*Males may learn 200 or more songs over the course of their lives.

*Mockingbirds sing throughout the day and often into the night, especially during a full moon.

Anglerfish

13

*Anglerfish live in complete darkness at the bottom of the Antarctic and Atlantic oceans.

*They have an antenna-like filament extending from their head used to attract prey.

*There are over 200 known species of Anglerfish
